BLACK

Black is a versatile color that can have multiple meanings for example it can represent Sophistication, formality, power, fear, mystery, death, evil, aggression, authority, rebellion and mourning. In middle eastern cultures the color can represent mourning and also rebirth. Black is also considered the opposite of white not only in the color spectrum but in also in good vs evil, light vs darkness, yin and yang and dark ages vs the age of enlightenment to name a few. Black has also been the symbolic color of authority and this is still being seen with judges today as they still wear black as their tradition dress.
