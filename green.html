Green

In my opinion the color green represents life, when looking at the color green I associate it with not only life and good health, but also island life as Hawaii and Puerto Rico have lush forest. There is a wide spectrum of thoughts and feeling associated with the color green. For instance, greed, war, sickness, wealth, luck and many more. The color green is also built into the DNA of some species of animals as a defense mechanism called camouflage. Which is used tp protect themselves from predators. Adopted by the military to serve a similar purpose. Green is a versatile as it can have different cooling or warming effect depending on the context in which it is used in. Due to the versatility that is why the color green makes it into my top 3 list of favorite colors.
